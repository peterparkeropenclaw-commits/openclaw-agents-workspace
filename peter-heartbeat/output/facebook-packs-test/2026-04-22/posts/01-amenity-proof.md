# Amenities only help when the guest can feel them

Creative headline: A long amenities list is not persuasive if the page does not show what the stay actually feels like.
Topic: amenity-proof

More amenities do not automatically create more bookings.

That is especially useful in UK markets where a lot of listings share similar baseline features.

Differentiation does not come from saying you have amenities.
It comes from making the practical value of them obvious.

The page should not just list conveniences. It should make them feel real.

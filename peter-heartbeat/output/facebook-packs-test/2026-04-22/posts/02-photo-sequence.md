# The photo sequence is part of the sale

Creative headline: A strong lead image helps, but the next few frames decide whether trust keeps building.
Topic: photo-sequence

Most hosts think about photo quality more than photo sequence, and that is where a lot of trust is lost.

I see this a lot with solid UK listings.
The property itself is fine, but the order shows the wrong rooms too early, repeats similar shots or leaves basic clarity until too late.

That makes the stay feel harder to understand than it really is.

A smart sequence usually does four jobs in the first few images:
sell the stay, confirm quality, reduce uncertainty, and make the layout easy to picture.

Photo order is not admin. It is part of the booking journey.

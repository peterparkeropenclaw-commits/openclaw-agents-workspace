# A full calendar is not the whole scorecard

Creative headline: Occupancy can look healthy while margin and positioning quietly leak underneath it.
Topic: occupancy-margin

Hosts sometimes celebrate occupancy when the better question is how expensive that occupancy was to win.

There is a big difference between filling nights because the offer is strong and filling nights because the price felt too easy to accept.

Both can produce a busy diary.
Only one tends to protect margin over time.

That is why occupancy on its own can be misleading, especially outside peak demand windows.

Busy is good. Busy at the right margin is better.
